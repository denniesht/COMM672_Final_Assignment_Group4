Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Our FAQ Page

Here you will find answers to the most common questions we receive. If you can't find what you're looking for, please use our contact form for more information.

  • Do I need prior volleyball experience to join?

    No prior experience is necessary. We welcome beginners as well as experienced players.

  • What equipment do I need to bring?

    Players should wear comfortable athletic clothing, non-marking court shoes, and bring a water bottle. Knee pads are recommended but not required.

  • How can I register my child for the club?

    You can register your child online through our registration page or by visiting us during practice hours for a paper registration form. For more details, please contact our support team.

  • Does La Jolla Volleyball offer Financial Aid or scholarships?

    We do receive some grant money and donations (varies year-to-year), which we distribute among those who apply and qualify for partial financial aid. Visit our Financial Aid page for more information.

  • What age groups does La Jolla Volleyball Club serve?

    La Jolla Volleyball Club offers programs for girls and boys from ages 8 to 18.

  • What are the practice schedules?

    Practice schedules vary by team and age group. Detailed schedules are available on the team pages and are updated regularly.

  • Can my daughter/son “play up” a division?

    We prefer that the concept of “playing up” be the rare exception, not the expectation. However, since we are a small club, there will be instances where we may ask certain players to play up to help fill out a team if the numbers of players do not work out exactly in each age division.

  • Where are the practices held?

    Practices are held at various locations in the La Jolla area, including local schools and community centers. Check the practice locations page for more information.

  • What do my LJV registration fees cover?

    Gym rentals, coaching fees, uniform, tournament entry fees, club insurance, administrative expenses, equipment, registrations. Depending on the level of team, amount of travel, or season of competition, certain teams may also receive additional uniform items and/or backpack, membership to SportsRecruits and/or Hudl, Recruiting Seminars, additional fitness workouts/practices, and/or participation medal/keepsake.

  • May I help coach my daughter or son during their match?

    Trust your child’s coach for the season. Avoid giving instructional comments at tournaments. Be supportive with positive remarks like “great effort!” or “way to go for it!” to encourage your child’s growth and enjoyment. Remember, volleyball is a fast-paced, imperfect game, and making mistakes is part of the learning process. The coach will guide your child to learn from these experiences.

  • Who are the coaches?

    Our coaches are experienced and certified professionals. Learn more about our coaching staff on the coaches page.

  • How can I get involved as a volunteer?

    We welcome volunteers to help with various aspects of the club. Please visit our volunteer page to see current opportunities and sign up.

  • What is La Jolla Volleyball’s policy regarding playing time?

    We ensure every player gets a chance to contribute in each match, so no one sits out a full 3-set game. If your daughter doesn't play in the first set, she'll play in the second. For younger teams, playing time is more equal if attendance, attitude, and effort are consistent. As players advance, playing time becomes more position-specific and may not be equal, but will always be fair and in the team's best interest.

  • May I get a refund if my daughter/son quits the team?

    We do not offer refunds for players that decide to discontinue or reduce their participation. At the beginning of each season, we carefully budget all club expenses (gym rental costs, tournament entry fees, uniforms, insurance, coaching stipends, etc). This budgeted amount is based upon a certain number of players paying their fees. These expenses remain fixed throughout the season, and do not go down if less players participate. Even if a player chooses to stop playing on their team, they are still responsible for their full season fees. Thank you for understanding our no refunds policy.

  • Are there any circumstances where LJV will offer a refund?

    Yes. In rare and unforeseeable circumstances we will consider a prorated refund, such as a season-ending injury or a pandemic. When COVID-19 forced our club to shut down in Spring 2020, we paused remaining payments, and processed prorated refunds to all participants.
